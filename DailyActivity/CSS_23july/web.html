indian history
history is a long and complex narrative, encompassing millennia of diverse cultures, empires, and religious developments. It begins with the Indus Valley Civilization, one of the world's oldest, followed by the Vedic period where Hinduism emerged. Various dynasties shaped the subcontinent, including the Mauryan and Gupta empires, with the latter often called the "Golden Age". Islamic influence grew from the 12th century onwards, with the Delhi Sultanate and Mughal Empire playing significant roles. paleolithic
The Paleolithic, or Old Stone Age, was the earliest period of human history, characterized by the use of rudimentary stone tools and a hunter-gatherer lifestyle. This era, spanning from approximately 3.3 million years ago to around 10,000 BCE, saw early humans adapt to various environments and develop basic technologies for survival. They primarily lived in small bands, relying on hunting, fishing, and gathering plants for sustenance.
