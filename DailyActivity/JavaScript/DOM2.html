A computer is a high-speed, electronic data processing device that stores and processes information to perform tasks like calculations, communication, and information retrieval. It consists of hardware components such as a monitor, keyboard, and CPU, and runs on software to execute specific programs and applications. Computers have become essential in virtually every aspect of modern life, from education and healthcare to business and entertainment, making tasks easier, faster, and more efficient.
